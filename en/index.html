Welcome to TECFA

Technologies de Formation et Apprentissage - Educational Technologies

TECFA is an academic unit specialized in the field of educational technologies, since 1989. This unit is part of the Faculty of Psychology and Educational Sciences (FPSE) from the University of Geneva, Switzerland, and is lead by Prof. Mireille Bétrancourt.

Research at TECFA covers a broad field of studies, such as cognitive, social and affective implications of information and communication technologies.

TECFA offers courses about educational technologies in a Master of Sciences in Learning and Teaching Technologies (MALTT), in a continuing education diploma in conception and development of e-learning projects, and in bachelor level classes. Platforms and pedagogical resources are also developed and maintained by TECFA.

Moreover, TECFA proposes services, participate to events and hosts online resources aimed at all age groups.
