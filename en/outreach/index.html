Outreach

TECFA shares its expertise in the area of training technologies and learning by proposing services, organising or participating to public events, as well as through public online resources.

Services

CeLEN : Consultation in eLearning and Numeric Education

Within the Pôle Cité of the Faculty of Psychology and Educational Sciences, CeLEN proposes three kinds of services:

  • Consulting for conception and assessment of e-learning devices;
  • Scientific expertise of e-learning projects;
  • Tailormade trainings in the area of educational technologies.

Visit CeLEN website (FR)

Public Events

Brown Bag Seminars

Organized once a month or so, Brown Bag Seminars host presentation of research or teaching technologies for learning, collaboration, communication, etc.

Brown Bag Website

Gaming Thursdays

Every two weeks or so, join us for the presentation of a video game by amateurs, with demo. From 12:30 to 13:30, room S01 of Uni Pignon. Any interested amateur can present a game !
